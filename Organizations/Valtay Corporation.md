# ®️ Valtay Corporation

## Who they are
[[Carl]] received a [[Valtay Corporation Neural Enhancer 544. Variant 32.c]] and a [[Valtay Corporation Neural Enhancer 275. Variant 35.j]].

Toward the end of [[Book 1 - Dungeon Crawler Carl]], [[Carl]] asks [[Odette]] about the [[Valtay Corporation]], which he's heard mentioned (it's not specified by whom/what). [[Odette]] reacts cautiously, warning him to be careful: it's a corporate government, one of the most powerful entities in the universe, which had a fleet ready to intervene to collect a debt owed by the [[Borant Corporation|Borant]] system — an intervention that was averted only because the kua-tin moved up the start of the game's season. They're described as a parasitic life form that favors aquatic hosts (like the kua-tin), and their version of the show would apparently focus more on android technology and pulse weapons than on fantasy creatures. They'll be the ones running the next season.
