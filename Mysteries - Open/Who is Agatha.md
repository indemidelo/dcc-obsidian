# ❓ Who is Agatha
## What we know
During the fight against the first-floor boss, [[Agatha]] levels up without having fought or completed the tutorial, simply by being present in the room — a behavior that surprises Carl. After the battle, the [[Meadow Lark]] group notices that her picture doesn't appear among the mugshots shown on the show's screen, unlike every other participant. When Carl asks [[Mordecai]] why Agatha was "edited out" of the battle broadcast, he downplays it, saying they probably didn't want to confuse the narrative — but Carl senses he's hiding something.
