# ❓ Mordecai's past
## What we know
[[Odette]] asks Carl to pass a message to Mordecai, offering him a job working for her. Mordecai reacts very badly to the offer, with harsh words about Odette. When Carl reports his reaction back to her, Odette admits — without going into detail — that she "pushed him too hard" in the past, and that he "paid the consequences" for it. She says she hopes he calms down and that she's willing to explain herself to him, if he'll let her.

## Open questions
- What exactly happened between the two of them?
- How did Mordecai "pay" for it?
- What does Odette want from him now, and why now?
- What is [[Mordecai]]'s past?
- What happened with [[Chaco]]?
