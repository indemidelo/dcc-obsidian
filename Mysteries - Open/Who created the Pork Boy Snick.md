# ❓ Who created the [[Pork Boy Snick]]
After the on-air confrontation between [[Carl]] and [[The Maestro]], a video (a "snick") starts circulating through the galaxy's social tunnels, heavily mocking the Maestro and comically exaggerating Carl's proportions. It goes viral within hours and triggers a reaction from the [[Skull Empire]]'s royal family. Carl notices the video seems made by someone more interested in hitting the Maestro than him, and wonders — without reaching a conclusion — whether the dungeon's own system AI might be behind it.
## Open questions
- Who actually made the video?
- Did the system AI play a role, as [[Carl]] suspects, or is it just his impression?
- Why target [[The Maestro]] specifically?
