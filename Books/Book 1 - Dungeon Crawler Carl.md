# 📖 Book 1 - Dungeon Crawler Carl

## Plot summary
One winter night in Seattle, a sudden transformation kills anyone who was indoors instantly. [[Carl]], freshly dumped by his ex-girlfriend, is outside to get the cat [[Donut|Princess Donut]] and survives because of it. The two find themselves inside an eighteen-floor "World Dungeon" that Earth has been turned into, run by the alien company [[Borant Corporation|Borant]] on behalf of a galactic governing body, the [[Syndicate]]. Guided by their Guildmaster [[Mordecai]], Carl and Donut take on the first floor, meet a group of survivors from a nursing home (including the elderly [[Agatha]]), defeat the first floor's boss, get pulled into TV interviews on [[Odette]]'s show, adopt a baby dinosaur (Mongo), and prepare to head down to the third floor, where they'll have to choose a race and a class.

## Things to remember
- At the end of the book, Odette hints at a "morally ambiguous" choice available only with high charisma during the class selection on the third floor, one that "will make someone angry" — not yet explained.
- The show's cameras always shut off whenever kua-tin are present (the species [[Zev]] belongs to), a detail that might connect to the fact that [[Agatha]] is never shown on camera — but the Book 1 text doesn't explicitly link the two.
