### Main Plot

Carl, Donut, and Mongo enter the Third Floor (the Over City), an urban environment with a fake starry sky. Mordecai transforms into an Incubus (level 50). Donut picks the **Former Child Actor** class and the **Manager** perk (Mordecai becomes their permanent manager). Carl picks the **Primal** race and the **Compensated Anarchist** class.

### Main Events

1. **Race and Class Selection**: Donut stays a cat, Carl becomes a Primal. Mordecai becomes their manager (and gets angry about it).
    
2. **Grimaldi's Circus**: They meet Signet (a level-60 Elite), who uses them to attack the circus. Carl discovers a City Boss (Grimaldi, level 85) and poisons him, then strikes a deal with the producers of _Vengeance of the Daughter_.
    
3. **Mongo**: They find a Velociraptor (Mongoliensis) in a Reward Room. Mongo becomes Donut's pet.
    
4. **The Sex Workers Quest**: GumGum (an orca) gives them a quest about dead prostitutes. GumGum is killed by the Krasue.
    
5. **The City Elves (201st Security Group)**: A cult that protects the Skyfowl, believing them to be angels.
    
6. **Miss Quill and Remex**: Miss Quill (the magistrate's assistant) is the true mastermind. She uses Remex (her husband, a Soul Leech Capacitor) to gather power and unleash _The Final War_.
    
7. **The Explosion**: Carl traps the explosion's gem in a glass case (Sheol Glass Reaper Case), creating **Carl's Doomsday Scenario**.
    
8. **The Escape**: They run for the Fourth Floor stairs along with other crawlers. Katia (a doppelganger, Monster Truck Driver class) joins the party.
    
9. **The Interview**: Odette reveals they would have received a Celestial Box, but Borant vetoed it. 83 crawlers received Celestial Boxes instead of them.
    
10. **Rankings**: Carl and Donut break into the Top 10.
