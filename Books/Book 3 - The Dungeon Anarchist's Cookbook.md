## Things to remember

Carl, Donut, Katia, and Mongo enter the **Fourth Floor (The Iron Tangle)**, a massive underground rail system. The floor is a maze of colored trains that only go one direction (upward), with transfer stations at prime numbers. To get out, they need to find the stairs at 12, 24, 36, 48, 72, and 433.

### Main Events

1. **The Train**: They start out on a Red Line train, full of monsters (Drek, Jikininki). They find a dwarf conductor (Vernon) who explains the system.
    
2. **The Reward Room**: Donut picks Mongo (a Velociraptor) in a reward room.
    
3. **Valtay and the neural implant**: Carl receives an implant from Valtay Corporation that lets him see through portals.
    
4. **The Dungeon Anarchist's Cookbook**: Carl receives a secret book from the Prize Carousel (after Mordecai gets put in timeout for attacking Chaco). The book contains recipes for explosives, potions, and information about the dungeon's universe.
    
5. **Mongo**: The dinosaur grows quickly, reaches level 15, and becomes an adult.
    
6. **Katia**: She becomes a permanent party member. She develops her "tank" build using a mass-metal inventory.
    
7. **The Train Quest**: They use the trains to save crawlers who are trapped. They discover the secrets of the rail system (the Abyss, the train yards, the portals).
    
8. **Hekla**: Tries to kill Katia with invisible arrows to anger Carl and take Donut. Katia accidentally kills her with Rush, becoming one of the strongest crawlers.
    
9. **Bully**: They meet the talking goat (ranked number 2) and Miriam Dom.
    
10. **Grull/Maestro**: Carl summons the war god Grull (possessing the Maestro) to kill a Mimic Rex threatening the train yard. The Maestro survives but is humiliated.
    
11. **Hekla's Death**: Donut turns Hekla's corpse into a minion, causing chaos among her daughters.
    
12. **The Fifth Floor**: They choose the "Air" quadrant and the "Dirigible Gnomes" opponents. Loita takes Zev's place as their PR agent.
